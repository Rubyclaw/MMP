Mandy Meyer Photography strives to provide all facets of photography. It has been a passion of mine ever since I can remember.

I do both in-studio and on-location photo sessions by request. My services include but are not limited to:

  • High School and College Portraits
  • Children, Family, and Individual Portraits
  • Events such as Graduations and Weddings
  • Nature and Travel Photography

Payment Methods: Mandy Meyer Photography accepts Personal/Business Checks and Cash. Invoices and/or receipts are provided for your records. Session Fees are payable at the time services are rendered. Payment for print orders are due when the prints are delivered. Please note: Session and Print Pricing are subject to change at any time, however Session Fees are locked in when you book your date and time. Print orders will reflect the current pricing at the time of your order.
